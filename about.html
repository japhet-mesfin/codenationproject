Our Mission...

Established in 2020, our mission is to serve the best haircuts to our community and beyond. Newly refurbished, with a widescreen TV, surround sound and individual screens in each booth, Manny's of London takes the haircut experience to a new level. Whether it's a high fade you're after or a shape up, the guys at Manny's can cater to whatever haircut you're looking for.
